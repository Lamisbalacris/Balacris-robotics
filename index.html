About Us

We are the Balacris team, the first robotics team in Al Jabal-Alakhdar region. The team was created by Eng. Iman in 2021 and is currently managed by three of the team members. We aim to spread the knowledge of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) in Libya, particularly in Al-Bayda City and Al Jabal-Alakhdar region.

Achievements

  • 1st place in LY LQ FTC 21-22
  • 2nd place in LY LQ FTC 22-23
  • 2nd place in LY RC FTC 21-22
  • Think Award in the regional championship in Tripoli
  • 1st place in Benghazi off-season

Participations

The team participated in a couple of festivals such as OMU Pharmacy Exhibition, OMU Agriculture College Festival, and Al Jabal-Alakhdar Exhibition for Communication and Telecom.

Future Goals

Our goal is to spread the knowledge of STEM in Libya and show the world that the new generation in Libya has goals to develop the country. We were selected among 100+ teams to perform at the First Global Challenge (FGC) as the Libyan National Team with 192 countries in Athens, Greece.
